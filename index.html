KASHMIR

Known for its breathtaking natural beauty, Kashmir is celebrated for its snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, and pristine lakes. Popular spots include Srinagar's Dal Lake, the picturesque Gulmarg, and the tranquil Pahalgam.

KOLKATA

The cultural capital of India, Kolkata is renowned for its rich literary heritage, colonial architecture, and vibrant arts scene. Landmarks include the Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, and the bustling streets of Park Street.

AGRA

Home to the world-famous Taj Mahal, Agra is a city steeped in Mughal history. It’s known for its impressive architecture, including the Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri, making it a must-visit for history enthusiasts.

MEGHALAYA

Located in the northeastern part of India, Meghalaya is known as the "Abode of Clouds." It’s famous for its stunning landscapes, including living root bridges, waterfalls, and caves. Shillong, the capital, and Cherrapunji, one of the wettest places on Earth, are key attractions.

DELHI

he capital city of India, Delhi is a vibrant metropolis known for its rich history, diverse culture, and iconic landmarks like the Red Fort, India Gate, and Qutub Minar. It is a blend of modernity and tradition, offering bustling markets, historical sites, and a thriving food scene.

UDAIPUR

Often called the "City of Lakes," Udaipur in Rajasthan is famed for its beautiful palaces, serene lakes, and romantic ambiance. Key attractions include the City Palace, Lake Pichola, and the majestic Jag Mandir.
